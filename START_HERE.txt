📊 SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE

Your Question
    ↓
Converted to vector (384 dimensions)
    ↓
Searched in FAISS index (1,675 items)
    ↓
Top 5 most similar notices retrieved
    ↓
Context built from notices
    ↓
Sent to ChatGPT (if key available)
OR
Local smart response generated
    ↓
Answer returned to browser
    ↓
Displayed in chat + Spoken aloud
