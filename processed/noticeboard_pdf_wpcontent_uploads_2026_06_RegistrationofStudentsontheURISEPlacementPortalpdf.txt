Registration Of Students On The Urise Placement Portal
June 1, 2026

Ms. Neha Srivastava                       Dr. A.P .J. Abdul Kalam Technical University Deputy Controller of Examination                                            Uttar Pradesh, Lucknow                                                                                                     Sector-11, Jankipuram Extension,    Ref: AKTU/CTPC/2026/1372 25 June, 2026 To, The Directors/Principals All Affiliated Institutions of AKTU, Uttar Pradesh Subject: Registration of Students on the URISE Placement Portal Sir/Madam, All students of the University are hereby informed that the URISE Placement Portal has been developed to facilitate access to placement opportunities, internships, career-related services, and recruitment drives. In order to avail these opportunities and remain updated regarding placement activities, all students are advised to register themselves on the URISE Placement Portal at the earliest using the link provided below: Registration Link: https://urise.up.gov.in/student/register   Steps for Registration 1. Click on the registration link mentioned above. 2. Select "AKTU" as your University. 3. Enter your Roll Number. 4. Enter your Date of Birth (DOB). 5. Click on "Register" to complete the registration process. All Directors/Principals of affiliated and constituent institutions are requested to disseminate this information among students and ensure maximum student registration on the portal latest by 30 June 2026. Students are advised to treat this matter as important and complete their registration at the earliest.  Best wishes,                     (Neha Srivastava)  Copy to:  (1) Registrar, AKTU, Lucknow (2) Finance Officer, AKTU, Lucknow (3) Staff Officer to Hon’ble  Vice Chancellor for kind information      (Neha Srivastava)
